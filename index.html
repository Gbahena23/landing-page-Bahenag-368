Keep Your Kicks Looking Fresh!

Our shoe cleaner is designed to deliver powerful cleaning for sneakers, leather, and more.
It effectively removes dirt and stains while restoring the original shine and color of your shoes.

HOW IT WORKS:

Spray:

Evenly apply the shoe cleaner across the surface of your shoes. Its powerful yet gentle formula penetrates deep to break down dirt, grime, and stains without damaging the material. Whether you’re cleaning leather, suede, canvas, or mesh, it’s safe and effective for all types.

Scrub:

Using the included premium brush, gently scrub the shoes in a circular motion.
The soft bristles lift away embedded dirt while protecting the fabric’s integrity.
For stubborn stains, give extra attention but always be gentle to maintain the shoe's texture and color.

Wipe & Shine:

After scrubbing, use a clean microfiber cloth to wipe off the residue. This step not only removes loosened dirt but also buffs the surface, restoring the shoe’s natural shine and vibrancy. Your shoes will look refreshed and rejuvenated, just like the day you bought them!
